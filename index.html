UI/UX Design

In this project, I spearheaded the UI/UX redesign of FitFlow, a mobile fitness app plagued by high churn rates from its cluttered interface and unintuitive workout tracking. Starting with user research—interviews with 40+ fitness enthusiasts and surveys revealing key pain points like overwhelming data visualization—I crafted personas, empathy maps, and journey flows to refocus on motivation and simplicity.

Android App

Android App

I developed the Android version of FitFlow, a high-performance fitness tracking app built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose to deliver a fluid, native experience for over 50K active users. Leveraging MVVM architecture, Coroutines for seamless background syncing, and Room for offline-first data persistence, I integrated real-time workout tracking with Wear OS devices and Google Fit API.
